About Me

I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, co-advised by Heng Ji (NLP) and Huimin Zhao (Chemical Engineering). My research focuses on AI for Science, including protein language model interpretability, natural language processing for bioengineering, and computational cancer genomics.
